== Description ==

Academy lite is an elegant and modern education WordPress theme. Academy lite WordPress theme is beautifully design and well coded theme can be useful for educational institutes, colleges, university, tution classes and all kind of educational purpose. Weather you are college professor of college or president of university you must need your institutional website looks and professional so visitor can play and decide to trust on their educational need. Academy lite theme gives your website clean and professional looks can attract visitors to your website. This theme is well coded for responsive mode so you can get proper look on any devices. This theme is comes with the theme customizer where you can use the homepage slider and sections by selecting pages from the dropdown it also has color options so you can choose the theme color as per your need. This theme is tested with latest version of WordPress and also compatible with popular plugin like woocommerce and contact form 7.
